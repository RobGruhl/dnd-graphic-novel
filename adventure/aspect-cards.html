HOW TO PLAY
Using Your Aspects

Each turn, pick TWO cards:

★ PRIMARY ASPECT: Your main action. Use its full effect.

☆ SECONDARY ASPECT: Supporting action. Half effect OR +1 to primary.

Example Combos:

  • Mighty Blow + Bless = Blessed strike (+2 to hit and damage)
  • Magic Bolt + Sneak = Surprise magical attack!
  • Healing Light + Shield Wall = Heal while protected
