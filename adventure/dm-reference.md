## Monster Quick Stats

### Trial 1: Dire Wolf (CR 1)
| AC | HP | Attack | Special |
|----|----|--------|---------|
| 14 | 22 | +5 to hit, 1d10+3 bite | Knocks prone on hit. **Pack Tactics** = advantage if ally nearby |

**Behavior when cursed/wounded:** Snarling, limping on one leg, dark energy crackling around a wound. Not attacking unless approached within 10 ft.

**Resolution options:**
- Fight: Standard combat
- Animal Friendship (DC 12): Wolf calms, follows at distance
- Healing Light: Removes curse, wolf becomes loyal ally for rest of adventure
- Sneak past: DC 14 stealth (wolf's passive perception 15)

---

### Trial 2: Goblin Archers

**4 Goblin Warriors (CR 1/4 each)**
| AC | HP | Ranged | Melee | Special |
|----|----|----|-----|---------|
| 15 | 10 | +4, 1d6+2 | +4, 1d6+2 | **Nimble Escape:** Bonus action Disengage or Hide |

**1 Goblin Boss (CR 1)**
| AC | HP | Attack | Special |
|----|----|--------|---------|
| 17 | 21 | +4 x2, 1d6+2 | **Redirect Attack:** Uses ally as shield. **Nimble Escape** |

**Setup:** Goblins in ruined tower (30 ft high, 60 ft away). Merchant wagon pinned down below.
- 3 goblins on tower
- 1 goblin + boss on ground level harassing merchant

**Resolution options:**
- Direct assault: Warrior approach, but hard due to distance
- Precise Shot/Magic Bolt: Pick them off from cover
- Sneak + flank: Get behind tower (DC 12 stealth)
- Battle Cry from cover: DC 12 morale check or goblins scatter
- Illusion: Fake threat draws them out

**Reward:** Merchant offers choice - gold (5gp), a weapon, food, or information

### Trial 3: The Enchanted Maze

**2-3 Animated Flying Swords (CR 1/4)**
| AC | HP | Attack | Special |
|----|----|--------|---------|
| 17 | 14 | +4, 1d8+2 slash | Immune to poison, psychic. Blindsight 60ft. |

**Hidden Friend: Pseudodragon (CR 1/4)**
| AC | HP | Attack | Special |
|----|----|--------|---------|
| 14 | 10 | +4 x2, 1d4+2 bite | **Magic Resistance.** Sting: DC 12 Con or poisoned/unconscious 1 hr |

**Maze Challenges:**
1. **Shifting Floor:** Tiles rearrange. Track DC 13 to find pattern, or Detect Magic auto-succeeds.
2. **Illusion Wall:** Fake dead end. Detect Magic reveals, or Investigation DC 14.
3. **Flying Sword Room:** 2-3 swords animate when center is crossed. Can be avoided with Sneak DC 15.
4. **Riddle Door:** "I have cities but no houses, forests but no trees, water but no fish. What am I?" (Answer: A MAP)

**Pseudodragon location:** Hiding in a alcove past the riddle door, injured. If the Player helps (healing, kind words, food), it bonds with him.

### Trial 4: The Wounded Pegasus

**Pegasus (CR 2) - ALLY, not enemy**
| AC | HP | Attack | Special |
|----|----|--------|---------|
| 12 | 59 (currently at 15) | +6, 1d6+4 + 2d4 radiant | Celestial. Understands Common, Celestial, Elvish, Sylvan. |

**Curse manifestation: 2-3 Shadows (CR 1/2)**
| AC | HP | Attack | Special |
|----|----|--------|---------|
| 12 | 27 | +4, 1d6+2 necrotic + Str drain | **Vulnerable to radiant!** Amorphous. Sunlight = disadvantage. |

**Situation:** Pegasus pinned down by dark magic, shadows circling. Pegasus can't fly until curse lifted.

**Resolution:**
- Fight shadows: Smite and radiant damage very effective (vulnerable!)
- Healing Light on Pegasus: Partially breaks curse, pegasus can help fight
- Protection Circle: Shadows can't enter, buys time
- Track/Detect Magic: Find the curse anchor (dark crystal nearby)
- Destroy crystal: DC 13 attack or Telekinesis to shatter it

**If helped:** Pegasus offers to carry the Player to the final trial and becomes ally.

### Final Trial: The Specter

**Specter (CR 1) - The Jealous Spirit**
| AC | HP | Attack | Special |
|----|----|--------|---------|
| 12 | 22 | +4, 2d6 necrotic + HP max reduction | Incorporeal. Resistant to almost everything. Sunlight = disadvantage. |

**Story:** Spirit of a previous Choosing candidate who failed and died bitter. Wants to steal the Player's potential.

**Setup:** Specter has captured/threatened the beloved NPC from Act 1. Found in a dark shrine.

**Phases:**
1. **Approach:** Use skills from earlier trials to reach the shrine
2. **Confrontation:** Specter taunts, reveals its story
3. **Combat/Resolution:**
   - Direct attacks work but specter is resistant
   - Turn Undead: Very effective (forces it away from NPC)
   - Divine attacks deal full damage (not resistant to radiant)
   - Healing Light on NPC: Weakens specter's hold
   - Compassion approach: If the Player tries to help/forgive the specter, it may find peace

**Victory:** NPC saved, specter defeated or redeemed, class crystallizes.

## Adaptive Difficulty

**If the Player is struggling:**
- Reduce enemy HP by 25%
- Have an ally appear (wolf, pegasus)
- Let creative solutions auto-succeed
- Divine Guidance gives very clear hints

**If the Player is breezing through:**
- Add one more enemy
- Enemies use tactical positioning
- Add a time pressure element
- Require combo solutions
