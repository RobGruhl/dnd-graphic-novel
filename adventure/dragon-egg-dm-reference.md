## EMBERHEART (Roleplay Only)

*Young Copper Dragon - NOT A COMBAT ENCOUNTER*

**Personality:**
- Grieving mother, desperate and hopeful
- Copper dragons love humor and riddles (but she's too sad for jokes now)
- Warm and affectionate to those who help her
- Recognizes Lightsword's draconic heritage immediately

**Key Lines:**
- "I smell... scales. Dragon-touched."
- "Gold and copper share the flame, little cousin."
- "Will you bring back my Cinderscale?"
- "I cannot leave. If I leave, these two will die."

**Stats (if somehow needed):**
Use Young Copper Dragon from Monster Manual - CR 7, AC 17, HP 119
But she will NOT fight while protecting eggs.

## THE EGG - CORRUPTION TIMER

**Rounds to Failure:** 3 rounds after combat starts (or when Ashscale completes his chanting)

**Visual Cues:**
- Round 1: Black thorns tighten, glow dims slightly
- Round 2: Thorns pulse with dark energy, egg makes distressed sound
- Round 3: Cracks appear in shell - LAST CHANCE

**To Save the Egg:**
- Fire damage (Lightsword's Fire Bolt) burns away thorns
- Radiant damage severs corruption
- Psychic damage (Spore's blade) cuts the magical connection
- DC 13 Arcana to know this, or just let them try fire logically

**If Egg is Corrupted:**
- Baby dragon hatches angry and confused
- Can be calmed with DC 15 Animal Handling
- Emberheart is devastated but doesn't blame the heroes
- Long-term: corrupted wyrmling becomes a future plot hook
