The Aspect Cards

In the Trials of the Choosing, young heroes receive a deck of Aspect Cards representing the four paths: Warrior, Hunter, Arcane, and Divine. How often each aspect is used during the trials determines the hero's final class. Lightsword's heavy use of Divine and Arcane cards, combined with natural magical ability, revealed him to be a Sorcerer with Draconic heritage.
