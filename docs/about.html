About This Project

The Story

"Lightsword: The Trials of the Choosing" is the origin story of a Lizardfolk Sorcerer, created as a graphic novel adaptation of an actual D&D session. The story follows a young hero through four trials, where choices made during the adventure determine their ultimate class and abilities.

Beginning as a glowing, indeterminate figure, our hero must prove themselves through acts of compassion, courage, and wisdom. Along the way, they gather four companions: Darkstorm the dire wolf, Pip the pseudodragon, Starwind the pegasus, and their best friend Spore the myconid.

How It Was Made

This graphic novel was generated entirely using AI tools:

  • Story Source: Original D&D campaign session transcript
  • Script Writing: Claude (Anthropic) adapted the session into comic panels
  • Image Generation: Google Gemini 3 Pro Image
  • Page Assembly: Python + Pillow for layout and packaging
  • Web Reader: Custom HTML/CSS/JavaScript comic reader

The Aspect Cards

In the Trials of the Choosing, young heroes receive a deck of Aspect Cards representing the four paths:

  • Warrior (Red): Strength, combat, protection
  • Hunter (Green): Stealth, precision, nature
  • Arcane (Blue): Magic, illusion, enchantment
  • Divine (Gold): Healing, holy power, blessing

How often each aspect is used during the trials determines the hero's final class. Lightsword's heavy use of Divine and Arcane cards, combined with natural magical ability, revealed him to be a Sorcerer with Draconic heritage.
