Locations

From underground caves to celestial temples, explore the world of the Trials of the Choosing.

Underground Cave Village

Starting Area

Lightsword's home deep beneath the earth. A cozy cave bedroom beside an underground river teeming with blind cave fish. Soft bioluminescent mushrooms provide gentle light. Where Lightsword and Spore share their friendship before the Choosing.

Temple of Aspects

Sacred Site

A magnificent temple complex with four towers representing the Aspects: red for Warrior, green for Hunter, blue for Arcane, and gold for Divine. The central courtyard hosts the Choosing ceremony where young heroes receive their Aspect cards and begin their trials.

The Four Pillars

Tutorial Area

Four glowing pillars in the Temple courtyard, each demonstrating an Aspect. The red pillar summons a training dummy, the green reveals illusory bushes, the blue creates a spinning crystal puzzle, and the gold presents a shadow over a flower.

Whispering Valley

Trial 1

A misty valley crossed by an ancient stone bridge over a rushing river. Wind whispers through twisted trees. The Cursed Dire Wolf guards the bridge, blocking passage until a hero shows compassion over combat.

Ruined Tower Clearing

Trial 2

A crumbling stone tower surrounded by overgrown ruins. Once a watchtower, now a goblin ambush point. A merchant's wagon is trapped here, arrows thudding into wood as goblins attack from the tower's heights.

Maze of Echoes

Trial 3

A labyrinth of crystal walls that shift and change. Illusions mask dead ends while real paths appear blocked. Features a sword chamber with animated flying blades, and hidden alcoves where injured creatures hide. Pip the pseudodragon waits here.

Cursed Grove

Trial 4

A once-beautiful forest clearing now twisted by shadow magic. Dead trees claw at purple-tinged sky. At its center, Starwind the pegasus is bound by chains of shadow, circled by three malevolent Shadows. Requires a powerful divine intervention to break the curse.

Temple Inner Sanctum

Final Battle

The heart of the Temple of Aspects, corrupted by the Shadow Sorcerer. Once a place of starlight and hope, now filled with creeping darkness. The altar where Spore is held captive. Where the final confrontation takes place and the transformation occurs.

Temple Courtyard (Restored)

Finale

The Temple courtyard returned to glory after the Shadow Sorcerer's defeat. The four towers glow bright again. Villagers gather to witness the Choosing ceremony as Lightsword is revealed as a Sorcerer with Draconic heritage.
