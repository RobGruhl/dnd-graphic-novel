# Lightsword: The Trials of the Choosing

A graphic novel telling the origin story of **Lightsword**, a Lizardfolk Sorcerer with Draconic Sorcery.

---

## CHARACTER VISUAL DESCRIPTIONS

### Main Character

#### **Lightsword** (Pre-Transformation)
**Physical Description:**
- A glowing, indeterminate humanoid figure wrapped in soft golden-white light
- No defined species - ethereal and mysterious
- Wearing simple ceremonial robes over the glowing form
- Eyes are bright points of warm light

**Distinctive Traits:**
- Gentle, curious posture
- Warm and compassionate expression visible even through the glow
- Light pulses gently with emotions

#### **Lightsword** (Post-Transformation - Lizardfolk Sorcerer)
**Physical Description:**
- Lizardfolk with green-brown scales that shimmer with draconic heritage
- Lean, athletic build
- Golden-amber eyes with vertical pupils
- Small horn-like ridges along the brow

**Clothing & Equipment:**
- Simple adventurer's clothing suitable for cave dwelling
- A glowing blade of light manifests in hand when needed
- Aspect cards tucked in a belt pouch

**Distinctive Traits:**
- Scales occasionally shimmer with magical energy
- Confident but kind expression
- Natural leader's presence

---

## NPC VISUAL DESCRIPTIONS

### Key NPCs

#### **Spore** (Myconid Companion)
- Small mushroom creature, knee-height
- Pale cap with bioluminescent spots that glow soft blue
- Big, expressive dark eyes
- Releases colored spores based on emotion (gray = scared, rainbow = joy)
- Lightsword's best friend from the underground village

#### **The Elder** (Temple Guide)
- Ancient, ageless humanoid figure
- Robes that shimmer with red, green, blue, and gold threads
- Long white beard, wise and kind eyes
- Carries a staff topped with four colored gems

#### **Darkstorm** (Dire Wolf - Redeemed)
- Massive wolf, horse-sized, silver-gray fur
- Originally had purple-glowing chains around front leg (cursed)
- After healing: Noble bearing, intelligent amber eyes
- Loyal and protective stance

#### **Pip** (Pseudodragon)
- Tiny dragon, cat-sized
- Reddish-brown scales with iridescent wings
- Large, expressive eyes
- Tail with distinctive stinger
- Perches on Lightsword's shoulder

#### **Starwind** (Pegasus)
- Magnificent winged horse
- Pure white coat, wings of pristine white feathers
- Originally gray and weakened when cursed
- After rescue: Radiant, powerful, grateful

#### **Shadow Sorcerer** (Villain)
- Hooded figure in tattered dark robes
- Face is a hollow mask of who they once were
- Purple eyes burning with jealousy and rage
- Form flickers between solid and shadow
- Tragic, not evil - failed Choosing candidate

#### **Garrett** (Merchant)
- Older human man with gray beard
- Kind face, practical traveling clothes
- Merchant's wagon with various goods
- Grateful and generous
